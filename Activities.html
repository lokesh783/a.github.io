Family Rides

Dizzy-Disk

Climb aboard our spinning disk! With it’s outwards facing seats, it’ll get you in a spin as it glides back and forth whilst spinning you in circles. It’s one dizzy ride, and a great new addition in 2019 to our ride offering here at Oakwood Theme Park!

Snake River Falls

climb up the steps and choose between our speedy open slides and our twisted tubes. Hop into a boat (together or alone) and prepare for a splash at the end when you come out of these water flumes! To ensure the enjoyment of your day, this ride opens at 11am every day

Treetops

Go for a magical adventure through the woodlands surrounding the park. Set high up in the treetops, you’ll twist, bend, and drop through the tree canopy. As one of our most popular rollercoasters, this ride has been thrilling generations of families for nearly 30 years
